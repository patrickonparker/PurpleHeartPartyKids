Inflatable Safety Policy

THE MAXIMUM NUMBER OF CHILDREN ALLOWED ON AN INFLATABLE IS AS FOLLOWS:

Age Number of Children
2-8 8 Children
9-13 6 Children
14-17 4 Children
18+ 1-2 Adults

PLEASE ALLOW ONLY COMPATIBLE AGE GROUPS TO PLAY ON THE INFLATABLE AT THE ONE TIME. CHILDREN MUST BE SUPERVISED BY AN ADULT AT ALL TIMES.

RULES MUST BE EXPLAINED TO CHILDREN PRIOR TO USE.

RULES: PERSONS NOT FOLLOWING SAFETY RULES SHOULD BE EXCUSED FROM PLAY IMMEDIATELY.

  • Always operate under adult supervision. Inflatables must be in viewing range AT ALL TIMES.
  • Individuals with head, neck, back or other muscular-skeletal injuries or disabilities, pregnant women, small infants and others who may be susceptible to injury from falls, bumps or bouncing are NOT PERMITTED in the unit at any time.
  • EXTRA CAUTION and supervision is required when younger children are on the inflatable.
  • Ensure all participants are in groups of similar size whenever possible. It is recommended that older children should be warned that they can send the smaller ones flying if they bounce too close or could cause an injury if they come in contact or fall on them.
  • ABSOLUTELY NO flips, wrestling, or other forms of roughhousing or extreme horseplay are allowed in the equipment.
  • DO NOT allow participants to Bounce, Sit or Lay down near the doorway unless entering or exiting the bounder.
  • Do not bounce any closer than two feet from each other or within two feet from the door. Bouncers should not sit or lay down while others are bounding around them, as a jumping child could fall down on top of a sitting or lying child.
  • Do not dive or jump through the entrance or exit of the equipment. Our inflatables are equipped with a safety entrance. Bouncer must enter and exit the inflatables from a sitting or crawling position.
  • Do not allow running in and/or bouncing against the sides of the inflatable equipment.
  • Remove all shoes, eyeglasses and sharp or pinned jewelry. Dangling and hoop earrings are the leading cause of injury. Have shoes clearly placed well out of the way of the entrance.
  • No PETS, sharp objects, or hard toys allowed inside the bouncer.
  • Stay off netting at all times. Do not allow climbing or hanging from the interior or exterior of the equipment.
  • ABSOLUTELY NO FOOD, BEVERAGE, OR GUM is allowed in or on any inflatable equipment.
  • No ‚ÄúSilly String‚Äù allowed in or around inflatable equipment. Should Silly String come in contact with the equipment, it will cause irreparable damage and damage fees will be incurred.
  • In case of high winds (over 20mph) and/or lighting, have children exit equipment and unplug blower o deflate unit.
  • In case of accidental deflation have children exit equipment then check for power outage or intake blockage to blower unit or disconnection of the blower tubes. (See set up requirements below).
  • Do not allow use of the equipment if the surface becomes wet. Have the inflatable surface towel dried before resuming play.


SET UP REQUIREMENTS
Before the inflatable arrives, please turn off automatic sprinklers and do not water the grass the night before. Pick up all animal feces. Make sure the area is level and free of rocks, sticks, etc. Also, please do not water grass until inflatable is picked up.
The inflatable can be set up on grass (preferably), cement, asphalt, or other surface that is relatively flat. The unit must be setup within 100 feet of an electrical outlet. Extension cords cannot be doubled up and no items can be connected to that some outlet.The inflatable will be inflated before it is removed so that we can inspect and do a preliminary cleaning of the unit.

WEATHER POLICY
Our inflatables have a roof, which provides shade from sun and light rain. However, if the local weather is call for all day rain/storms or winds of 20 mph or more on your reserved day and both you and Purple Heart Party Kids, LLC agree before delivery that it may be unsafe to use, then we will cancel the rental without penalty.

PLEASE NOTE: NO REFUNDS WILL BE GIVEN AFTER THE INFLATABLE IS SET UP.
If the inflatable unit gets wet, everyone should exit the inflatable and unplug the blower unit. If the inside of the inflatable becomes wet it can become very slippery and can become dangerous. If the inside does become wet, dry with towels. Once dry, the inflatable should be once again safe for use.
